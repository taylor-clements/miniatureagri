ABOUT ME

My name is Taylor and i live in a very rural village in the countryside of Dorset

i have a huge passion for agriculture. Along with this i also have a passion for modelling, so i decided to put the two together and start collecting 1/32 scaled models of tractors and other agricultural machinery. I am now starting to venture into the world of "conversions" which is where you convert a model, making it look more detailed, weathered or even changing the whole look / brand of the model..
